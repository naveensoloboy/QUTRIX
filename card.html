GAIT :(Gobi Arts Association of Information Technology)


Gobi Arts Association of Information Technology is an association formed by the Department of Computer Science. It is a Forum through which the students improve their leadership qualities and organisational abilities. Guest Lectures, Seminars etc are also regularly organised through this outfit. GAIT conducts intercollegiate meet and intra collegiate meet (GAITFEST) every year.

Intercollegiate meet (QUTRIX)


It is the annual technical extravaganza and the state level technical symposium conducted during the month of March every year. Qutrix invites students of Computer Science from various Institutions and Universities of TamilNadu and Pondichery. This helps the students of Computer Science and the participants to improve the communication skills, organisational abilities, leadership qualities and develop the spirit of team work among them.

PAPER PRESENTATION :
  • One Team Per Department.
  • Maximum of Three Participants per team (Equal participation is must).
  • The paper must be IEEE format.
  • Full paper of minimum 6 pages with an abstract, not exceeding 250 words along with Hard Copies and One Soft copy through mail (qutrix2k2023@gmail.com) should be sent.
  • Title, Author Name, Mobile Number, E-mail id, College name (for further communication) abstract in a separate sheet
  • Paper Size :A4, Font Name: Times New Roman, Font Size: Heading-14, Sub Heading: 12+BOLD, Paragraph -12, Line spacing -1.5)
  • Paper should reach us on or before 11 September, 2023 along with a Bonafide Certificate
  • Selected teams will be informed through e-mail on 13 September 2023. Selected team should participate in the event and should give a Power Point Presentation at the time of registration

WEB DESIGN

SOFTWARE CONTEST
  • One team per Department.

  • 5 Teams Selected to Finals.
  • FINALS: Participants need to debug the code for the given set of problems In Any Language. The one who solves maximum number of problems correctly in the allotted time shall be declared as winner.

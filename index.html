Inaugurated On

    On 30th July,1968,the College was formally inaugurated by the then Minister for Education Dr.Navalar V.R.Nedunchezhiyan.

In the next year itself

    The College was upgraded as a first grade college with the introduction of B.A. Economics and B.Sc. Mathemathics.

College Ranking

    The college has been ranked 57th in the country by National Institute Ranking Framework(NIRK) in 2018.

College Funded under

    The college has been funded under the DBT Star College Scheme by Govt. of India during 2020

College Offers

     CA Foundation course is offered to the students who have completed +2 and also to the students currently pursuing any UG degree in our college from this academic year 2023 onwards.

Kasadara Technology Solutions

     Under Rural Innovations Center of Excellence,Gobi Techno Park is Inaugurated,through which Kasadara Technology Solutions is offering employment to our students from the academic year 2023 onwards.
